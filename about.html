Who We Are

Design Hive Interiors is a creative interior design and visualization studio based in Kisumu, Kenya. We specialize in transforming residential, commercial, and hospitality spaces through innovative design, technical expertise, and professional consultation.

Our Mission

To create functional, beautiful, and sustainable spaces that improve the quality of life and exceed client expectations.

Our Vision
